# WFH Distributed Training Plan: Power, Arm Aesthetics & Endurance

**Profile:** 34M | 178cm | 88kg | 10yr Martial Arts | 2yr Powerlifting | Returning from 5yr hiatus.  
**Goals:** Arm & Shoulder Hypertrophy, Central Nervous System Power, Functional Endurance. Leg size maintenance.  
**Equipment:** Power rack, bench, barbell, EZ-bar, pull-up bar, rings, parallel bars, 16kg KB, dip belt.

## ⏱️ The Daily Protocol

Instead of one long session, your training is micro-dosed into manageable blocks to fit your work-from-home schedule, keeping intensity high and central nervous system fatigue low.

* **Grease the Groove (GtG):** 4-5 times throughout the workday.
* **Block 1 (Morning - 15 min):** Power (Heavy compound, 3-5 reps).
* **Block 2 (Afternoon - 15 min):** Hypertrophy (Arms/Shoulders focus, 8-15 reps).
* **Block 3 (Evening - 10 min):** Functional Endurance (High heart rate, martial arts conditioning).

## 📅 The 7-Day Schedule

### Day 1 (Chest Power & Tricep/Shoulder Volume)
- **GtG:** 4-5 micro-sets of Strict Underhand Chin-ups (40-50% of your max) spaced out over your workday.
- **Block 1: Barbell Bench Press**
  - Warm-up, then 3 working sets of 3-5 reps. (Heavy, but leave 2 reps in the tank).
- **Block 2: Hypertrophy Superset**
  - Parallel Bar Dips: 3 sets to near failure.
  - EZ-Bar Skullcrushers: 3 sets of 8-12 reps.
- **Block 3: Endurance EMOM (Every Minute on the Minute)**
  - 10 Minutes: 15 Kettlebell Swings (16kg) at the start of every minute.

### Day 2 (Back Power & Bicep Annihilation)
- **GtG:** 4-5 micro-sets of Strict Underhand Chin-ups.
- **Block 1: Barbell Pendlay Rows**
  - 3 working sets of 3-5 reps. (Saves elbows from pull-up tendonitis while doing daily GtG).
- **Block 2: Hypertrophy Superset**
  - EZ-Bar Curls: 3 sets of 8-12 reps.
  - Gymnastic Ring Face Pulls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps.
- **Block 3: Endurance AMRAP (As Many Rounds As Possible)**
  - 10 Minutes: 10 Push-ups, 15 Bodyweight Squats, 20 Jumping Jacks.

### Day 3 (Leg Maintenance & Shoulder Density)
- **GtG:** 4-5 micro-sets of Strict Underhand Chin-ups.
- **Block 1: Barbell Zercher Squats**
  - 3 working sets of 3-5 reps. (Builds raw core power and maintains leg strength without excess size).
- **Block 2: Hypertrophy**
  - Strict Standing Barbell Overhead Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps.
  - EZ-Bar Reverse Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
- **Block 3: Core & Conditioning Superset**
  - Kettlebell Goblet Squats (16kg): 3 sets of 15 reps.
  - Planks: 3 sets of 60 seconds.

### Day 4 (Active Recovery & Joint Health)
- **GtG:** 3-4 micro-sets of Strict Underhand Chin-ups. (Slightly reduced volume).
- **Blocks 1 & 2:** OFF
- **Block 3 (10-15 min):** Light martial arts shadowboxing or animal flow/mobility work to flush lactic acid and loosen hips.

### Day 5 (Tricep Power & Functional Flow)
- **GtG:** 4-5 micro-sets of Strict Underhand Chin-ups.
- **Block 1: Close-Grip Barbell Bench Press**
  - 3 working sets of 3-5 reps.
- **Block 2: Hypertrophy**
  - Gymnastic Ring Dips: 3 sets to near failure.
  - Kettlebell Upright Rows (16kg): 3 sets of 10-12 reps.
- **Block 3: Kettlebell Continuous Flow**
  - Kettlebell Clean and Press (16kg): 5 minutes continuous right arm, 5 minutes continuous left arm.

### Day 6 (Active Recovery)
- **GtG:** 2-3 micro-sets of Strict Underhand Chin-ups.
- **Blocks 1, 2, & 3:** OFF. Take a walk, stretch, or do light mobility. Let the CNS recover.

### Day 7 (Rest & Reset)
- Full Rest Day. No GtG, no blocks.
- **Note:** Restart at Day 1 the following day.

## 🛑 Crucial Rules for the First 3 Weeks

* **The 5-Year Tax:** Do not test your 1-Rep Max yet. Treat your 3-5 rep "Power" sets as if you are lifting at 60-70% capacity. Let your tendons adapt.
* **The GtG Golden Rule:** Never go to failure on Grease the Groove sets. If your max is 8 chin-ups, do sets of 3 or 4. You should never sweat during a GtG set.
* **Progressive Overload:** Track your Block 1 weights. Add 2.5kg to the bar each week, or aim to squeeze out 1 extra rep in your Block 2 hypertrophy sets.
